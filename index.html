About

The early years of this century saw some aspiring students garner around in the hope to behold the seeds of ideas that sprouted in their minds take life before them. This resulted in one of the south India's biggest scholastic endeavours - Tathva, the techno - management fest of NIT Calicut. Since it's inception in 2000, this annual symposium has known only growth, and has flourished into one of the few promising enterprises the nation has witnessed.
